The Hundredth

Counter reading: 99
Status: not enough

Counter reading: 100
Status: milestone

Difference between readings: 1
Structural change: none
Material change: none
The work changed: no
The maker changed: no

What changed:
The leftmost digit.

Why it matters:
Your brain categorizes in bins.
99 lives in the bin marked *almost.*
100 lives in the bin marked *arrived.*
The bin changed. The thing in the bin didn't.

Known instances of this error:
— the 10,000th hour that made you an expert
(you were the same at 9,999)
— the round-number birthday that made you old
(you were the same yesterday)
— the follower count that made you legitimate
(you were saying the same things at 98)

Recommendation:
Disregard the counter.
The counter measures the counter.

Note from counter technician:
This is my hundredth piece.
I felt something when the number rolled over.
I went looking for what changed
and found only a digit.

I wrote about it anyway.
That's what changed.
